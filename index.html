Water efficiency in African data centers

This project explores how energy mix, climate conditions and water usage efficiency interact in African data centers, using interactive dashboards to reveal environmental trade‑offs and highlight more sustainable infrastructure choices.

This mini-suite of dashboards lets you move from high-level country summaries to detailed seasonal dynamics and energy mix evolution. Each view reuses the same data backbone for consistent comparisons.
