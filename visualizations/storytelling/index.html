An environmental study by Edgar DEMEUDE, Guilhem DUPUY, Vincent JOULAIN, Anh Vu DUY.

Climate Context

Before analyzing the energy impact, it is essential to understand the climatic constraints to which infrastructures are subjected (temperature, humidity, water scarcity).

Water Consumption of Data Centers

Water usage in Data Centers is multifaceted: for cooling systems, everyday usage for restrooms, landscaping, staff needs, and especially the energy production required for their operation.

Understanding Seasonality

How climate affects water usage

The efficiency of water used directly for cooling (WUE "Cold Water") is not constant. It varies according to local climatic conditions. By observing monthly averages, clear trends emerge:

  • Summer Peaks: An increase in cooling WUE during hot and humid seasons.
  • Winter Stability: Cold periods show optimal efficiency (lower values).
